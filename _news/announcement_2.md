### :dart: Who We Are Looking For

**We do not use GPA as the sole filter.** A transcript tells us how well you take exams, but **code tells us how well you build the future.**

Here is what we value the most:

- **Passion is Priority #1.** You must be genuinely interested in **Embodied AI, Robotics, or Computer Vision**. We want students who are curious enough to read papers for fun and persistent enough to debug a model.

- **Hands-on Capability > Exam Scores.** We value "builders." You should be comfortable getting your hands dirty with code and systems. We prefer students who have deployed models on real robots or participated in coding competitions (ACM/ICPC, Kaggle) over those with perfect grades but zero practical experience.

- **Sharp Execution ("Get Things Done").** We value efficiency and professionalism. In our lab, being "capable" means you can take ownership of a problem, break it down, and execute the solution without needing hand-holding at every step.

- **Logical & Critical Thinking.** Research is about asking the right questions. We appreciate students who can think logically, challenge existing assumptions, and propose reasonable hypotheses.

---

### :hammer_and_wrench: Technical Prerequisites

While we welcome students from diverse backgrounds, the following skills will help you survive and thrive in our group:

- **Programming**: Proficient in **Python** (C++ is a strong plus). Familiarity with Linux environments.
- **Deep Learning**: Experience with **PyTorch** or TensorFlow. You should know how to train a network from scratch.
- **Mathematics**: Solid intuition in Linear Algebra, Probability, and Calculus.
- **English**: Ability to read and summarize CVPR/ICCV/ECCV papers efficiently.
